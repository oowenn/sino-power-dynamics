Experience, Capability, and Delivery

Experience

Our team includes electrical engineers with experience in the U.S. power industry. They provide customized design solutions for customers and help our manufacturing partners in China understand U.S. requirements, so products meet IEEE, ANSI, and NEMA standards. We also provide U.S.-based service, and our China operations bring over 30 years of experience in the U.S. market.

Capability

Sino Power Dynamics works with three production bases in China, each manufacturing a different class of electrical product: Sanbian Technology (20kV to 550kV), Changzhou Pacific (20kV to 345kV transformers and switchgear), and Shuangxin (6kV to 110kV distribution transformers).

Delivery

Production capacity has increased significantly, improving delivery times: about 3.5 months for 550kV units, 3 months for 35kV to 220kV units, and 1 month for distribution transformers. In 2024, capacity was expanded with a new factory at Shuangxin, along with larger reserves of core materials such as silicon steel.

Product lines

Sino Power Dynamics offers two main product lines. Switchgear is also available through our manufacturing network.

Power Transformers

33kV – 550kV

Oil-immersed power transformers manufactured for data centers and other key projects, designed to meet IEEE, ANSI, and NEMA standards.

Lead time: 3 months (33–220kV) · 3.5 months (550kV)

Distribution Transformers

6kV – 33kV

Oil immersed and Dry-type, cast-resin distribution transformers, manufactured at our Distribution Transformer (DT) production base.

Lead time: as fast as 1 month

Switchgear (35kV–220kV) is also available through our manufacturing network. Contact us for details.
